Therac-25

A Machine Used to Save Lives, Ends Up Taking Lives

The Therac-25 is a radiation therapy machine used to help treat and kill cancer. In this case at least six people were over-exposed to radiation causing injury and even one documented death. To summarize how the machine works, it uses two types of radiation, electron-beam, and x-ray converted electron-beam. The important part to understand is that the x-ray electron-beam was high power and supposed to be concentrated to a direct area. There was software precautions to prevent patients from getting an over exposure of radiation, but with a complex user-interface the operator and the machine manual did not provide proper documentation about error messages. So the operator had no idea of the harm the machine was causing.
