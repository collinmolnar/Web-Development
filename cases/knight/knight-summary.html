Knight Capital

The Costliest Computer Bug Ever!

Imagine just losing $440 million dollars in one day. Well it happed, to Knight Capital Group. Knight is a financial service firm which is the largest trader in U.S equities, and it takes a lot of software and computers to make that all happen. On August 1, 2012 a computer bug caused Knight to complete several times more trades than usual in a day, causing a major disruption in the stock market and Knight's equities. Although several corporations provided financial support to prevent Knight from going bankrupt, it is still scary to think that one bug could cause a collapse of the financial system!

Not many details of the software errors have been released to the public. I believe this is because Knight has a reputation to keep. Companies will try to keep bugs as unknown as possible because they may lose credibility and business.
